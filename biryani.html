Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500 grams chicken
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons biryani masala
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Water

Instructions:

  1. Wash and soak the rice in water for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a large pot. Add onions and cook until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  4. Add chicken, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until chicken is browned.
  5. Add tomatoes and cook until they are soft.
  6. Stir in yogurt and biryani masala. Cook until the oil separates from the mixture.
  7. In a separate pot, bring water to a boil. Add soaked rice and cook until itâ€™s 70% done.
  8. Layer the partially cooked rice over the chicken mixture. Garnish with fresh coriander and mint leaves.
  9. Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes.
  10. Serve hot with raita or salad.
