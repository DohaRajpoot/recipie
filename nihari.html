Nihari

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg beef or mutton, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons nihari masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2-3 cloves
  • 1-2 cardamom pods
  • 1 stick cinnamon
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking
  • Water

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a large pot. Add onions and cook until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  3. Add beef or mutton pieces and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in yogurt, nihari masala, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook for a few minutes.
  5. Add water to cover the meat. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until the meat is tender.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or paratha.
